About ShareTools

Building stronger communities through tool sharing

Our Mission

ShareTools connects neighbors through a simple idea: why should everyone own a power drill that they use twice a year? We believe that sharing tools, equipment, and resources makes communities stronger, saves money, and helps the environment.

Our platform makes it easy to borrow what you need and share what you have, creating connections and reducing waste in the process.
